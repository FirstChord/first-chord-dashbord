# First Chord Dashboard - Music School Management System
Full-stack admin platform streamlining operations for 160+ students across 12 tutors.

## Problem Solved
Managing schedules, payments, student progress, and tutor coordination for a growing music school was becoming impossible with spreadsheets and manual processes.

## Key Features
- **Student Management**: Comprehensive profiles, lesson history, progress tracking
- **Tutor Dashboard**: Schedule management, student notes, payment tracking
- **Automated Billing**: Integration with payment systems
- **Real-time Updates**: Live sync across all tutor devices
- **Analytics**: Business insights and student progress metrics

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: JavaScript, HTML/CSS
- Backend: Node.js/Express (assumed)
- Database: SQL/NoSQL integration
- APIs: Payment processing, calendar sync

## Impact
- Reduced admin overhead by 70%
- Improved tutor-student communication
- Enabled data-driven business decisions
- Scaled operations from 50 to 160+ students

## Business Value
Direct revenue impact through improved efficiency and customer retention.

*Currently powering First Chord Music School's day-to-day operations.*
